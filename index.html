Experience Royal Indian Dining

Where tradition meets timeless elegance











The first floor of the restaurant exudes timeless luxury and grandeur. Golden ornate detailing covers the ceilings, pillars, and mirrors, creating a royal ambiance. Soft, warm lighting from elegant wall sconces enhances the rich gold tones throughout the space. Intimate tables dressed in crisp white linens offer a refined and romantic dining setting.


The second floor presents a spacious and well-organized dining environment with a refined traditional touch. Intricate ceiling designs and elegant chandeliers create a bright yet graceful atmosphere. Wooden partitions neatly divide the seating areas, offering both privacy and structure. Detailed carvings and decorative wall panels reflect classic craftsmanship and heritage design.
